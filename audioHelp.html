How to Upload Custom Audio

You MUST have Simple Voice Chat installed to hear the custom music discs.
1. Run "/audioplayer web" and copy your token.

2. Open the Audio Upload resource.

3. Paste your token into the text box.

4. Upload your audio.

5. Hold your item and click the "[Put on Item]" button in your chat.

6. Run the command.

7. Play your disk/goat horn/etc.
